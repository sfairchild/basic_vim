##  history

* QED ("quick editor") - 1965 and 1966
  * ed (The first editor made for UNIX) - 1969
    * em (editor for mortals) - 1976
      * en - 1976 - Bill Joy and Chuck Haley
        * ex ("en extended") - 1977 - Bill Joy and Chuck Haley
          * vi ("visual") - 1977
            * vim ("Vi IMproved") - 1991
